## 🚀 Version 2.0: AI Academic Operating System
*Target Release: Q3 2026*

The focus of Version 2.0 is to expand ShieldAI into an active, deep-knowledge learning assistant capable of mapping complex subject material automatically.

### 1. Document Parsing & Syllabus Decomposition
* **Multi-Format Extraction**: Support PDF, Word, Powerpoint, and OCR-based image uploads.
* **Knowledge Mapping**: Extracts core themes, academic timelines, dependencies, and complex reading tracks from lecture notes.

### 2. Spaced Repetition Core
* **Recall Intervals**: Automatically structures active recall and review sessions based on Leitner algorithms, pushing study prompts before topics drift from memory.
* **Smart Flashcards**: Automatically extracts complex definitions and creates active recall decks.

### 3. Smart Mock Examination Engine
* **Contextual Test Generation**: Automatically drafts realistic test questions of varying formats (Multiple Choice, Short-Answer, Essay Prompts) modeled directly on syllabus topics.
* **Performance Evaluations**: Evaluates completed student answers, scores them, and identifies concept weaknesses.

## 🌐 Version 3.0: AI Life Operating System
*Target Release: Q1 2027*

Version 3.0 transitions ShieldAI from a desktop workspace into a holistic, health-conscious executive life companion.

### 1. Biometric Wearable Integrations
* **HRV Stress Tracking**: Connects to Apple Health, Garmin, or Fitbit SDKs. Monitors real-time Heart Rate Variability (HRV) and elevates task pacer-break frequencies automatically if acute physiological stress is detected.
* **Sleep Optimization**: Consumes sleep score data to adjust study schedules—proactively suggesting low-effort research tasks when sleep scores drop.

### 2. Live Peer Focus Rooms (Multiplayer Sprints)
* **Team Work Sprints**: Group focus tables with real-time video, study audio channels, and shared, synchronous breathing breaks.
* **Procrastination Accountability**: Integrated companion avatars that visually react to help peers stay focused.

### 3. Integrated Enterprise Sync
* **Bidirectional Synchronization**: Seamless background synchronization across Google Calendar, Microsoft Exchange, Slack, and Zoom.
* **Schedule Shifting**: If an unexpected high-stress meeting block is added, upcoming focus intervals are shifted to preserve flow state.
